Project Overview

The Challenge

Gaia needed a more refined and cohesive digital presence to match its fashion identity. The challenge was to create content that felt stylish, modern, and aligned with current trends while maintaining a clear brand direction.

The goal was to elevate the brand visually and position it as more distinctive and recognizable within a competitive fashion space.

What I Did

Strategy & Execution

I developed a visual direction focused on aesthetics, styling, and consistency. The content was designed to feel curated and aligned with fashion trends while maintaining brand identity.

By improving both visuals and communication, the brand achieved a stronger and more polished presence that better connects with its audience and reinforces its positioning.

Project Overview

The Challenge

NewDermaPro needed a stronger digital presence that felt both credible and visually aligned with its skincare identity. The challenge was to create content that looked polished, communicated trust, and helped the brand stand out in a crowded market.

The goal was not just to make the page look better, but to build a clearer brand image through strategy, consistency, and stronger communication.

What I Did

Strategy & Execution

I developed a clearer content direction for NewDermaPro by aligning the visual output with the brand’s skincare identity. The focus was on creating a feed that felt polished, trustworthy, and consistent, while making the overall presence more relevant to the target audience.

Alongside the visual direction, I helped shape the communication strategy behind the content by refining how the brand presented its value, products, and tone. The result was a stronger digital presence that felt more intentional, professional, and audience-aware.

Project Overview

The Challenge

Symphony Beirut needed a stronger online presence that could clearly communicate its educational value while remaining visually engaging. The challenge was to simplify complex messaging and make the brand feel more accessible and modern.

The goal was to create content that connects with the audience while building trust and clarity across all communication channels.

What I Did

Strategy & Execution

I refined the brand’s communication approach by making its messaging clearer and more audience-focused. The content strategy was built to highlight value while maintaining a consistent and professional visual identity.

The result was a more structured content direction that improved clarity, strengthened engagement, and positioned the brand more effectively within its space.
